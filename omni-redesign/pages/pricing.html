GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I really start for free?

Absolutely! Every plan comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You can explore all the features and build your first landing page before committing to a paid plan.

What happens after my free trial ends?

After your 14-day trial, you can choose to upgrade to any paid plan to keep your pages live. If you don't upgrade, your pages will be paused but not deleted. You can upgrade anytime to reactivate them.

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, the change takes effect at your next billing cycle.

Is there a long-term contract?

No contracts ever. All plans are month-to-month unless you choose annual billing (which saves you 20%). You can cancel anytime with no penalties or hidden fees.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes! We offer a 25% discount for registered non-profit organizations. Contact our sales team with proof of your non-profit status, and we'll set you up with a special pricing plan.

What if I need help getting started?

We've got you covered! All plans include access to our knowledge base and video tutorials. Professional and Business plans also get priority support. Business plan customers even get custom training sessions with our team.
