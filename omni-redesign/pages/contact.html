Common Questions

What are your support hours?

Our support team is available Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 6 PM Eastern Time. Business and Enterprise plan customers also have access to weekend support. Email support is monitored 24/7, and most emails are answered within 2 hours during business hours.

Do you offer custom training for teams?

Yes! Business plan customers receive complimentary custom training sessions for their team. We can tailor the training to your specific use case and workflow. Contact our sales team to schedule a session.

Can I schedule a demo before signing up?

Absolutely! We'd be happy to show you around the platform and answer any questions. Schedule a personalized demo with our team using the form above, and we'll reach out within 2 hours to set up a time that works for you.
